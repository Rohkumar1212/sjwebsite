College Requirements

All admissions to the University are given by admission cordinators and official admission partners.

Candidates to make sure to submit list of documents to the consultants:-

  1. Aadhar card
  2. Photo
  3. Class 10 marksheet
  4. Class 12 marksheet
  5. Graduation marksheet if applicable

Candidates are requested to contact only official admission partners.
